New York City is known for its vast tourist sites and activities such as Times Square and watching Broadway plays. However, there are a vast number of small businesses and local activities that are a significant part of the culture of NYC. Visit the following places and explore what else NYC has to offer!
Click on the image to go to the site of the shop.


Mil Mundos Books

sells: books written in English and Spanish.
you can find: the only bookstore in Bushwick east of Myrtle Ave, with titles on the shelves available side by side in English and Spanish - the dominant language spoken in Bushwick.
if you enjoy: reading and exploring books from different cultures.
women owned.

Aqua

sells: fitness studio activities.
you can find: the best of high performance fitness with the ambiance of modern wellness.
if you enjoy: exercise and fitness.

Cafe Con Libros

sells: books and coffee.
you can find: a vibrant community space where everyone; specifically female-identified folx, feel centered, affirmed and celebrated.
if you enjoy: Starbucks at Barnes and Nobles vibes.
women owned.

Fish Eddy

sells: unique and quirky dishware and other home goods.
you can find: fun experience exploring quirky and fun designs on various dishes, glasses, and even paintings.
if you enjoy: Urban Outfitters.
women owned.
