At beneficium our goal is to not only help small businesses grow, but help YOU grow. If you have been wanting to start a small business and don't know where to start, please use the following resources to get help with where to start.
Good luck with your endeavors! We hope to see you join the community!


NYPL's Services

The NYC Small Business Resource Center at the Thomas Yoseloff Business Center offers myriad resources to help you start and grow your small business.

New York Small Business Development Center

The New York Small Business Development Center (NYSBDC) provides small business owners and entrepreneurs in New York with the highest quality, confidential business counseling, training, and business research at no cost.

NYC Small Business Services

The NYC Department of Small Business Services (SBS) helps unlock economic potential and create economic security for all New Yorkers by connecting New Yorkers to good jobs, creating stronger businesses, and building thriving neighborhoods across the five boroughs.
